About

iREADER is an android-based informal reading enhancement application by Antonio R. Naing Jr. which developed primarily to enhance the reading skills of Grade 2 pupils of Palanas Elementary School. It utilized android devices which are within the interest of school age. This app highlights different activities geared towards improving the reading skills associated with auditory discrimination,recognizing alphabet Reading skills associated with identification and classification of objects, reading/recognizing numeracy concepts and reading skills associated with sensory/perceptual domains.
